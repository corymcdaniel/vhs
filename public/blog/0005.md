# >> 0005 AI Projects: Chip Gather

*Do you also collect casino chips?*

## Purpose

[chipgather.com](https://www.chipgather.com/)

I try to save a $1 chip from casinos I go to.  Yes I enjoy gambling and yes I know many hate the concept.
Anyway, I wanted a way to digitally keep track of chips I've collected, especially after finding out that
at least a few other people do this.  Hopefully this can be something fun for many, but for now I'm just
trying to keep it light.

### BlackJack

I added a way to play some blackjack.  I am thinking to work on this more, but currently
you can play solo or with others.  Nothing to bet - maybe I'll do something with collected chips.
